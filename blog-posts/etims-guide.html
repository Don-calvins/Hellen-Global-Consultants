Introduction

The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) has revolutionized tax compliance with the introduction of the Electronic Tax Invoice Management System (eTIMS). This system represents a significant shift from traditional invoicing methods to a real-time, digital-first approach to tax reporting.

Whether you're a small business owner just hearing about eTIMS or an established enterprise looking to optimize your compliance, this comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

📌 Key Takeaway

eTIMS is mandatory for all VAT-registered taxpayers in Kenya. Non-compliance can result in penalties of up to KES 1,000,000 or imprisonment for up to 3 years.

What is eTIMS?

eTIMS (Electronic Tax Invoice Management System) is KRA's digital platform for generating, storing, and managing tax invoices in real-time. It replaces the old method of generating invoices and waiting until filing time to report sales.

Key Features of eTIMS:

  • Real-time Reporting: Sales data is transmitted to KRA immediately upon invoice generation
  • Automated VAT Returns: System automatically populates VAT returns based on issued invoices
  • Digital Record Keeping: All invoices are stored securely in the cloud
  • Integration Capabilities: Can integrate with existing POS and ERP systems
  • Mobile Accessibility: Generate invoices via smartphone using the eTIMS mobile app

Who Needs to Register for eTIMS?

The following taxpayers are required by law to use eTIMS:

  • All VAT-registered persons (both individuals and companies)
  • Businesses with annual turnover exceeding KES 5 million
  • Taxpayers who voluntarily wish to use the system for easier compliance

💡 Pro Tip

Even if you're not VAT-registered, consider voluntary eTIMS registration. It simplifies record-keeping and prepares your business for growth and future compliance requirements.

eTIMS Registration Process

1

Login to iTax

Access your iTax account at itax.kra.go.ke using your KRA PIN and password.

2

Navigate to eTIMS Menu

Go to the "Returns" menu and select "eTIMS Registration" from the dropdown options.

3

Choose Registration Type

Select either "New Registration" or "Migration from Old System" depending on your status.

4

Complete the Form

Fill in your business details, including business name, address, and contact information.

5

Select Integration Method

Choose how you'll connect to eTIMS: Direct integration, web interface, or mobile app.

6

Submit and Wait for Approval

Submit your application and wait for KRA approval, which typically takes 2-3 working days.

eTIMS Integration Options

1. Direct API Integration

For businesses with existing ERP or POS systems, direct API integration allows seamless connection between your system and eTIMS. This is the most efficient option for high-volume businesses.

2. Web Interface

KRA provides a web-based portal where you can manually enter invoices. Suitable for businesses with low transaction volumes.

3. Mobile App

The eTIMS mobile app allows you to generate invoices on the go using your smartphone. Perfect for small businesses and mobile service providers.

4. EFD Integration

If you already use an EFD machine, you can integrate it with eTIMS for automatic invoice transmission.

Daily eTIMS Compliance Requirements

What You Must Do Daily:

  • Generate invoices for all sales before or at the time of transaction
  • Ensure invoices include: KRA PIN, invoice number, date, customer details, item description, quantities, prices, and VAT
  • Transmit invoices to KRA immediately (or within 24 hours for offline scenarios)
  • Keep copies of all issued invoices

What You Must Do Monthly:

  • Review the sales summary in your eTIMS dashboard
  • Reconcile eTIMS sales with your internal records
  • File VAT returns by the 20th of the following month
  • Pay any VAT due by the same deadline

Common eTIMS Issues and Solutions

❌ System Downtime

Solution: Use offline mode to generate invoices, then sync when system is back online. Always keep backup records.

❌ Integration Errors

Solution: Check API credentials, ensure proper data formatting, and test in sandbox environment first. Contact your developer or our support team.

❌ Duplicate Invoices

Solution: Use the void function immediately if an invoice is generated in error. Keep records of all voided invoices.

❌ Slow Performance

Solution: Check your internet connection, clear browser cache, or try using the mobile app for better performance.

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Failure to register: KES 100,000 or imprisonment for up to 3 years
  • Late invoice transmission: KES 10,000 per invoice
  • Incorrect invoices: KES 20,000 per invoice
  • Failure to keep records: KES 50,000
  • System tampering: KES 1,000,000 or imprisonment

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use eTIMS if I'm not VAT registered?

A: Yes, voluntary registration is allowed and recommended for businesses planning to grow.

Q: What if I have no sales on a particular day?

A: You don't need to generate any invoices, but you should maintain a "nil sales" record for your own reconciliation.

Q: Can I issue manual invoices?

A: No, all invoices must be generated through eTIMS. Manual invoices are not recognized for VAT purposes.

Q: How long should I keep eTIMS records?

A: Tax records, including eTIMS invoices, must be kept for at least 5 years.

Q: What happens if I lose internet connection?

A: eTIMS has an offline mode. Generate invoices offline, and they will auto-sync when connection is restored.
